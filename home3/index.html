Our Methodology

Study Abroad Guide

Detailed Roadmap to your dream university abroad.

Profile Analysis+
Our counsellors review your academic data, extracurricular activities and professional experience to identify your strengths. We then guide you through crafting an impactful application to global universities.
University Selection+
Keeping your goals, qualifications, curriculum interests, and cultural preferences in mind, our experts help you select a country, university and programme that's the right fit for you.
Test Preparation+
Your test scores qualify you to pursue a degree from top universities like Harvard, MIT, Stanford, Yale, and more. EduQuest has guided thousands of students to reach their dream colleges.
Essays / SOPs / LORs+
Essays, Statements of Purpose, and Letters of Recommendation enhance your college application. A good essay is not about the quality of language but about you as a person and your story.
Interview Preparation+
Our interview specialists conduct recorded mock interviews with the most recent and commonly asked questions. Along with real-time feedback, we prepare you for both online and in-person interviews.
Funding Your Degree+
At EduQuest, we start by listing all possible scholarships a student can get based on their background and scores. Last year, our students were awarded more than $8 million in scholarships.
Post Landing Care+
Our dedicated team of international program coordinators provide adequate post-landing support to ensure the student goes through a smooth transition in a foreign land.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The EduQuest?+
We are an independent education consulting company that helps applicants plan their journey at the school, college or postgraduate level. At the heart of The EduQuest is our commitment to finding the 'best-fit' institution for every applicant.
What makes The EduQuest different from other advisory services?+
Highly qualified, diverse team with over 20 years of consolidated experience. Customised counsel for every applicant. Data-driven approach using technology. Extensive scholarship knowledge — $8M+ awarded last year. Ethical standards and highest professional practices.
When should I contact The EduQuest?+
For undergraduate studies, we advise parents and students as early as grade 8 on pre-application strategy. Undergraduate applicants in Class 11 should contact us at least six months prior to the regular deadline.
Does EduQuest guarantee scholarships?+
EduQuest has a proven track record of securing scholarships for students. Last year alone, our students received over $8 million in scholarships from universities across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Singapore.
What is the highest SAT score EduQuest students have achieved?+
Our students have achieved SAT scores of 1590–1600 (near perfect to perfect). We've also had students score 36 on the ACT (perfect score) and 5/5 on multiple AP subjects. Our 95% success ratio reflects the quality of our coaching.
